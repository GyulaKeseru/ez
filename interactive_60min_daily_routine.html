60-Minute Interactive Daily Routine Lesson

1. Bemelegítés (10 perc)

What time do you usually wake up?

What is your favorite part of the day? Why?

2. Olvasás és szövegértés (15 perc)

Read the text:

Every morning, I wake up at 7:00 AM. I brush my teeth, take a shower, and eat breakfast. Then, I go to work at 8:30 AM. In the afternoon, I have lunch at 1:00 PM and finish work at 5:00 PM. In the evening, I relax, watch TV, and sometimes read a book before going to bed at 10:30 PM.

What does he do at 1:00 PM?
